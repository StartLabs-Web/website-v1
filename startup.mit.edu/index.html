Frequently Asked Questions
How do I join?

Just contact us at startlabs-contact@mit.edu or show up to one of our weekly meetings!

What kind of events can I expect?

We host events like Pitch Night and E-Lounge (entrepreneurship lounge) where you can meet like-minded peers who like entrepreneurship and learn from experienced entrepreneurs!

What kind of services will StartLabs provide me?

We get access to the Martin Trust Center, and we have lots of resources for budding entrepreneurs!

Why should I join?

We are some of the coolest people you'll meet at MIT!

Let's make MIT a place to start innovating ideas for the future.
